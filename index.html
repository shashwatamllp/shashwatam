Welcome to Shashwatam

Shashwatam Eco-Chie Creation LLP is an export unit that manufactures and exports garments. Our specialty is high quality garments and timely delivery. We emphasize on manual marketing and our customers come directly from the B2B network.

Our Divisions

  • Shashwatam: Our garment export division
  • Kiccha: Kiccha is our kidswear brand which we make available on various online seller portals. Attractive design and durability is our promise. Every garment is made for the happiness of children.
  • Vedvit: Vedvit LLP is our software arm. We are working on various useful software solutions. Some new ideas are currently under research and require investment. As soon as the first prototype is ready, we are planning to launch it in the market.
