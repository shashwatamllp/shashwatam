About Us

Shashwatam Eco-Chie Creation LLP is a diverse business group focused on excellence in exports, fashion, and software innovation. Our mission is to deliver quality and satisfaction through timely services and innovative ideas.

Shashwatam

Shashwatam is our garment export division. We specialize in manufacturing high-quality garments for international markets. We focus on manual B2B marketing and build direct relationships with our customers. Timely delivery and customer satisfaction are our top priorities.

Kiccha

Kiccha is our kidswear brand. Our products are available on popular online seller portals. We combine vibrant designs with long-lasting quality. Each piece of clothing is created to bring joy to children and comfort to parents.

Vedvit

Vedvit is our software innovation wing. We are developing new and useful software solutions aimed at solving real-world problems. Several exciting ideas are in the research phase, and we are seeking investment for prototype development and market launch.
